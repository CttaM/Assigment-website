Task 1

CARP Task

The CARP task was assigned to us to develop our skills in presentation, specifically in posters. CARP is an ancronym meaning: Contrast, Proximity, Repition and Alignment.

The CARP task was the first task that we were assigned. We had to create three draft posters for the True/False festival. Then we had decide which one of our drafts we wanted to further improve. Click here to learn more about the task.
